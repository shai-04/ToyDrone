Introduction

This game was developed by Shaiyur Dooken for a technical assessment assigned by Games Global. This game was developed using HTML5, CSS3 and JS (vanilla, no frameworks here!). This assessment required the candidate to create a 10x10 grid which allows a toy drone / tank to be placed on. Once the drone is placed on the grid, the player can then have access to the following functions:

  • Movement
  • Rotation
  • Firing a missile
  • Report status

Placement

Before you can access any other function of the drone, it has to be placed on the grid first. To do so, simply tap or click the cell you'd like the drone to be placed in. Cells can be identified by the white border around it. Hovering over the cell will also cause it to be highlighted for easy identification.

Movement

Press the X key on your keyboard to advance the drone 1 cell in the direction that it is currently facing. For mobile players, press the button on-screen.

Rotation

Press the ⇐ key on your keyboard to rotate the drone 90 degrees to the left. Press the ⇒ key on your keyboard to rotate the drone 90 degrees to the right. Alternatively, for mobile players, press the or button on-screen to rotate 90 degrees to the left or right respectively. The direction can also be viewed on the dashboard compass.

Firing a missile

Press the Z key on your keyboard to launch a missile 2 cells ahead of the current direction that you're facing. Alternatively, press the button on-screen.

Report status

To view the current position and direction of the drone, press the C key on your keyboard, or the on-screen. The status will be displayed on the dashboard.
